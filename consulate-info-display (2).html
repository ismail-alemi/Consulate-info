OUR SERVICES
What we help with
The Consulate provides the following core services to applicants.

Visa Issuance

Tourist, Visit, Business, and Entry (Employment) visas for travel to Afghanistan.

Passport Services

Renewal, replacement, and travel document assistance for Afghan nationals.

Document Attestation

Attestation and legalization of personal, educational, and commercial documents.

Business Facilitation

Support for trade, export-import documentation, and business invitation matters.

General Consular Help

Guidance for Afghan nationals residing in or visiting Telangana and nearby states.
